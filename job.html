Job Opening

We are looking for qualified individuals to fill the position below:

  • Position: Minister of Religion

The successful candidate will be responsible for performing the following duties (but not limited to):

  1. Lead worship services
  2. Preach sermons from the Bible
  3. Conduct prayers and liturgy
  4. Provide spiritual guidance and counseling
  5. Visit and support the sick, elderly, and needy
  6. Administer sacraments (e.g., baptism, communion, weddings, funerals)
  7. Teach the beliefs of Christianity
  8. Lead Bible study and religious education programs
  9. Offer pastoral care to individuals and families
  10. Oversee church activities and programs
  11. Work with church leaders, staff, and committees
  12. Organize community outreach and charity work
  13. Promote unity and moral values within the congregation
  14. Provide leadership and vision for church growth

How to Apply

Interested candidates should send their CVs to admin@livingwordhull.org with the job title as the subject of the email.
